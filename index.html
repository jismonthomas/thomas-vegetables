About us

Thomas Vegetables opened in 1980 at Sreemoolanagaram, Kalady by the father of Thomas, Vareeth Thaliyan with one simple concept: To provide our community with freshly picked produce from local farmers.

Now even after 4 decades our concept remains same and is carried by the son of Vareeth, Thomas Thaliyan who has been running the store since 1989.
